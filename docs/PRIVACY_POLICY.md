# Privacy Policy

**HumbleHalal** — humblehalal.com
**Last updated:** 7 March 2026
**Data Protection Officer:** dpo@humblehalal.com

## 1. About This Policy

HumbleHalal ("we", "our", "us") is a platform operated in Singapore that helps the Muslim community discover halal food, businesses, events, travel, and services. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and your rights under the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act 2012 ("PDPA").

By using HumbleHalal, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. What We Collect

We believe in collecting only what we need. Here is exactly what data we hold, depending on how you use the platform.

### Browsing (No Account Required)

When you browse our site without an account, we do not collect any personal data that can identify you. We use an anonymous session identifier (a random code stored in a cookie) to understand how visitors use the site. This code cannot be linked to you as an individual.

### Community Account

When you create an account to write reviews, post in the forum, list classifieds, or save listings, we collect:

- **Display name** — a first name, nickname, or alias of your choosing. This is displayed publicly next to your reviews and posts. It does not need to be your legal name.
- **Email address** — used for account login and transactional notifications (e.g., "Your review has been published"). Managed by our authentication provider, Supabase.

Optionally, you may also provide:
- **Profile photo** — uploaded by you
- **Short bio** — written by you
- **General area** — a broad neighbourhood (e.g., "Tampines"), not a specific address

We do **not** collect: your full legal name, NRIC, phone number, home address, date of birth, gender, race, or religion at this stage.

### Bookings and Purchases

When you book a hotel or purchase event tickets through HumbleHalal, we collect additional information required to fulfil the transaction:

- **First name and last name** — needed by the hotel or event organiser to process your booking
- **Email address** — to send your booking confirmation and e-ticket
- **Phone number** (event tickets only, optional) — only if you opt in to WhatsApp event reminders

This transactional data is stored separately from your account profile and is automatically deleted 12 months after the transaction is completed.

We do **not** store your credit card number, bank details, or any payment card information. Payments are processed entirely by our payment partners (Stripe for event tickets, LiteAPI for hotel bookings) who maintain their own security certifications.

| Data | Purpose | Legal Basis (PDPA) |

| Anonymous session ID | Understand site usage patterns, improve the platform | Legitimate business purpose (no PII involved) |

## 5. Cookies

We use cookies to keep the site working and, with your permission, to understand how the site is used.

### Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These are required for the site to function. You cannot disable them.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `sb-access-token` / `sb-refresh-token` | Supabase authentication session | Session / 7 days |
| `hh_session` | Anonymous session identifier for site analytics (random hash, not PII) | 30 days |
| `cookie_consent` | Remembers your cookie preference | 365 days |

### Optional Cookies (Require Your Consent)

These only load if you click "Accept All" on the cookie banner.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose |
|--------|----------|---------|
| `_ga`, `_ga_*` | Google Analytics | Anonymous site usage analytics |
| `ph_*` | PostHog | Product analytics (anonymous) |

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the cookie icon in the site footer.

## 7. Your Rights

Under the PDPA, you have the following rights:

### Access Your Data

You can view and download all personal data we hold about you at any time via your account settings page. We will provide your data in a machine-readable format (JSON or CSV).

### Correct Your Data

You can update your display name, email, bio, area, and profile photo at any time through your account settings.

### Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw marketing consent (newsletter opt-out) at any time through your account settings or by clicking "Unsubscribe" in any marketing email. You may also withdraw consent for optional analytics cookies via the cookie settings in the site footer.

### Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account at any time through your account settings. When you delete your account:
- Your profile, display name, and email are permanently removed
- Your reviews and forum posts are preserved anonymously (your name is replaced with "[Deleted User]")
- Any active classifieds are removed
- Transactional data (booking names, emails) is immediately anonymised
- Your newsletter subscription is cancelled

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Make a Complaint

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@humblehalal.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) at https://www.pdpc.gov.sg.

## 8. Children

HumbleHalal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at dpo@humblehalal.com and we will promptly delete it.

## 9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, use, or disclosure:

- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- Authentication is handled by Supabase with industry-standard security practices
- Payment card data is never stored on our servers
- Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel only (the platform administrator)
- We use Sentry for real-time error monitoring and security alerting
- Database access is protected by Row Level Security policies

No system is 100% secure. If we discover a data breach that may affect you, we will notify you and the PDPC as required by law.
